Lalibela

Lalibela is a small town in the highlands of northern Ethiopia, a country that's been Christian since 330 A.D., making it the oldest Christian country in the world. It is also home to a truly awe-inspiring mystery: a series of 11 churches like no other, all built without bricks, mortar, lumber or concrete.
